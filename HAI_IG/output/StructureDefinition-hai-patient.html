D.4.1 StructureDefinition: hai-patient

D.4.1.1 Formal Views of Profile Content

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/hai/StructureDefinition/hai-patient

This profile is based on the US Core Patient Profile and defines HAI specific patient constraints: A patient identifier representing the facility-assigned patient ID is required. Other identifiers for the person can also be present, such as a United States Social Security Number (id/@root = 2.16.840.1.113883.4.1), a Medicare beneficiary identifier (id/@root = 2.16.840.1.113883.4.338), or secondary patient IDs (id/@root = the appropriate facility OID for patient IDs). The patient name is optional; consult the NHSN reporting protocol and requirements. TODO* - this is required for US Core Patient Patient gender and birthdate are required.

This profile builds on US Core Patient Profile.
