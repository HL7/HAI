Healthcare Associated Infection Report

Managed by Community Health and Hospitals

This profile further constraints the C-CDA on FHIR US Realm Header profile. It specifies the generic constraints for all NHSN Healthcare Associated Infection Reports. Further constraints are found in the specialization profiles for single-patient and population summary reports, and in the profiles for specific report types..
