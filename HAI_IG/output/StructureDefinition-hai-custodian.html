D.4.1 StructureDefinition: hai-custodian

D.4.1.1 Formal Views of Profile Content

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/hai/StructureDefinition/hai-custodian

Defines NHSN custodian constraints and extensions on the Organization resource for use in HAI reports. NHSN is the custodain of NHSN HAI Reports.

This profile builds on Organization.
